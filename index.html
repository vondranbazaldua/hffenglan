Our Beliefs

Three principles shape every release.

We design for the long term. That means refusing the easy wins — more telemetry, more notifications, more noise — in favour of software that respects your attention and your data.

Minimalist by intent

Every pixel, every animation, every dependency is questioned. We ship the smallest useful thing, then iterate.

  • No dark patterns, no manipulative copy
  • Hand-tuned transitions, zero generic templates
  • Designed to fade into your day, not interrupt it

Privacy as foundation

Local-first data handling, opt-in telemetry, encrypted backups. We treat privacy like an architectural decision, not a setting.

  • Data lives on your device by default
  • Zero third-party trackers in our apps
  • Public privacy & data-flow documentation

Engineered experience

Procedural generation, custom render pipelines, hand-crafted audio engines — we treat interaction as the product.

  • Procedural audio synthesis & local DSP
  • Custom UI & motion systems per platform
  • Hardware-accelerated graphics on iOS, macOS & Android
Stack Highlight

A real-time procedural engine — running entirely on-device.

From instrument practice analytics to film-style colour grading, our apps share a custom-built DSP and rendering core. No cloud round-trips, no per-user sampling, no latency.

  • Zero round-trip latencyHardware-accelerated synthesis and rendering with deterministic timing.
  • Truly localAudio, images, and personal data are processed on your hardware — even when offline.
  • Made for the long runBackward-compatible data formats, archive-grade export, no data lock-in.
